Vission
MIT aspires to be a leader in Techno-Managerial education at the national level by developing students as technologically superior and ethically strong multidimensional personalities with a global mindset.
Mission

We are committed to provide wholesome education in Technology and Management to enable aspiring students to utilize their fullest potential and become professionally competent and ethically strong by providing,

* Well qualified, experienced and Professionally trained faculty

* State-of-the-art infrastructural facilities and learning environment

* Conducive environment for research and development.

* Delight to all stakeholders.

Quality Policy
To strive for global standards of excellence in all our endeavors such as teaching-learning, consultancy, R&D etc., through the process of self-evaluation and continuous improvement.

Excellent Library

The MIT Central Library with its state-of-the-art building, hardware, software and other allied resources is fast developing into one of the finest libraries in Marathwada Region. Library is enriched with wide variety of books, well equipped with modern facilities and resources in the form of books, e-journals, CD-ROMs etc.

Rooftop hi –tech Research Farm

  • Department of Agricultural Engineering design & developed a Research farm on Institute building roof occupied 4000 sq.ft area.
  • Project includes 20 brass red soil with shade net structure.
  • Project having well established micro irrigation system includes water source, pump, solenoid valve, filters, fogger, lateral etc.
  • Department students utilized this farm for their mini projects, automation, modern irrigation practices and Irrigation system study. Group of students from different disciplines preparing and operated project start from bed preparation-crop management to marketing of harvesting product to gain the marketing knowledge of agricultural produce.
  • Student successfully grown and harvested strawberry, capsicum and other commercial crops since 2014.
